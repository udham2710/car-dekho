        Maruti Dzire

        Maruti Dzire is a 5 seater Sedan car. Maruti Dzire Price starts from ₹ 6.57 Lakh & top model price goes upto ₹ 9.39 Lakh. This model is available with 1197 cc engine option. This car is available in Petrol and CNG options with both Manual & Automatic transmission. It's Petrol mileage ranges between 22.41 to 22.61 kmpl & CNG mileage is 31.12 km/kg. Dzire has got 2 Star safety rating in global NCAP crash test & has 2 safety airbags. This model is available in 7 colours. Based on Maruti Dzire user reviews, it has earned an overall user rating of 4.3 out of 5.

        Maruti Dzire car latest update

        Latest Update: Get savings of up to Rs 30,000 on the Maruti Dzire this May.

        Price: The Maruti Dzire is priced from Rs 6.57 lakh to Rs 9.39 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).

        Dzire 2024: The new-generation Maruti Dzire could offer these 5 things over the existing Dzire.

        Variants: The Maruti Dzire comes in four broad variants: LXi, VXi, ZXi, and ZXi+. The VXi and ZXi trims also offer a CNG option.

        Colour Options: Customers can get it in seven monotone colour options: Oxford Blue, Magma Grey, Arctic White, Phoenix Red, Splendid Silver, Bluish Black, and Sherwood Brown.

        Boot Space: The Dzire has a boot space of 378 litres.

        Engine and Transmission: It is equipped with a 1.2-litre petrol engine (90 PS/113 Nm), available with either a 5-speed manual or a 5-speed AMT. For the CNG variants, the engine produces 77 PS and 98.5 Nm, available exclusively with a 5-speed manual transmission.

        Claimed Fuel Efficiency:

        • 1.2-litre MT: 22.41 kmpl.

        • 1.2-litre AMT: 22.61 kmpl.

        • CNG MT: 31.12 km/kg.

        Features: Key features include a 7-inch touchscreen infotainment system with support for Android Auto and Apple CarPlay, cruise control, automatic climate control, and a height-adjustable driver seat.

        Safety: Safety features include dual front airbags, ISOFIX child-seat anchors, and rear parking sensors. 

        Rivals: The Maruti Dzire competes with Honda Amaze, Hyundai Aura, and Tata Tigor.
