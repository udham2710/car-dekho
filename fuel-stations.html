        Frequently Asked Questions

        What types of fuel are available at petrol pumps?

        Mostly, petrol pumps offer petrol and diesel fuel, but some fuel stations also provide CNG and LPG.

        What are the operating hours of petrol pumps?

        Petrol pumps often operate 24 hours a day, seven days a week. However, some may have specific hours, so it's advisable to check the operating hours of a particular pump.

        Do petrol pumps provide other services?

        Yes, besides fuel, petrol pumps may offer other services such as tyre inflation, oil checks, pollution checks, and basic vehicle servicing.

        What safety precautions should I follow at a petrol pump?

        Don't smoke, turn off your vehicle's engine, avoid using your mobile phone, and follow all the safety guidelines mentioned at the fuel pump.
