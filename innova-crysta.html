        Toyota Innova Crysta car latest update

        Latest Update: Toyota has launched a new mid-spec GX Plus variant of the Innova Crysta that slots between the entry-spec GX and mid-spec VX trims.

        Price: The Toyota Innova Crysta is priced from Rs 19.99 lakh to Rs 26.30 lakh (ex-showroom Delhi).

        Toyota Innova Hycross: Toyota has launched a better equipped GX (O) petrol-only variant of the Toyota Innova Hycross. It is priced from Rs 20.99 lakh (ex-showroom) and is available in both 7- and 8-seater options. In related news, its fully loaded ZX and ZX(O) hybrid variants are available to book once again.

        Variants: The Innova Crysta is now available in four broad variants: GX, GX Plus, VX, and ZX.

        Colour Options: You can get the Innova Crysta in five monotone colour options: Platinum White Pearl, Super White, Silver, Attitude Black Mica, and Avant Garde Bronze.

        Seating Capacity: It offers 7- and 8-seater configurations.

        Engine and Transmission: The Innova Crysta is powered with a 2.4-litre diesel engine (150 PS and 343 Nm), paired with a 5-speed manual transmission.

        Features: Key features include an 8-inch touchscreen infotainment system withAndroid Auto and Apple CarPlay, an 8-way power-adjustable driver’s seat, automatic climate control with rear AC vents, and ambient lighting.

        Safety: Safety features include seven airbags, vehicle stability control (VSC), hill-start assist, front and rear parking sensors, and brake assist.

        Rivals: The Innova Crysta is a premium alternative to the Mahindra Marazzo and Kia Carens, and a diesel counterpart to the Toyota Innova Hycross and Maruti Invicto.
