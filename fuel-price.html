        Fuel Price in India Today

        Generally, petrol prices revise at 6 a.m. daily in India. Indian Oil, Bharat Petroleum, Hindustan Petroleum (HP), and Reliance are the popular fuel companies in India that have the most fuel stations.

        How is the petrol price calculated?

        Crude Oil: In India,fuel prices completely depend on the cost of crude oil imported from other countries.

        OMC costs: Oil companies refine crude oil into petrol or diesel and sell it to dealers in various states of India.

        Value-added tax (VAT): It differs from state to state, which is why fuel prices differ across different states and cities.

        Excise Duty: The excise duty is the same across all states on fuel.

        Dealer commission: This factor is also considered and varies from dealer to dealer.
